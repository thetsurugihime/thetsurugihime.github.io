About
Tsurugihime

A side-scrolling action RPG in a beautiful 3D world. The last battle is in 100 days. How you reach it, depends on the player. ”Craft” everything and beat the game how you want to. Slash through the army of enemies with countless swords in this action battle game. A wide variety and depth of customization: main character, sword, base, Imouto (sister), and much more. Multiple endings that change depending on how you spend your 100 days.
